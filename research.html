Research Interests

  • Fractional Differential Equations
  • Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)
  • Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)
  • Spatio-temporal Climate Data Analysis
  • Air Quality Prediction
  • Statistical Modeling & Quantile Regression
  • Lie Symmetry Analysis
  • Invariant Subspace Method
  • Mathematical Modeling

My research is focused on integrating mathematical theory with computational modeling to analyze and solve real-world problems. I specialize in fractional differential equations and deep learning models such as PINNs and GNNs for complex spatio-temporal systems like climate and air quality. I also apply symmetry methods and statistical techniques for model analysis and prediction.
